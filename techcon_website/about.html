History

TechCon began as a regional meetup for developers and has grown into an international conference connecting researchers, engineers and industry leaders. Over the years it expanded from a single track to multi-track programs covering AI, IoT, HealthTech and developer tooling.

Mission

Our mission is to accelerate knowledge-sharing and collaboration across technology disciplines, fostering responsible innovation and practical solutions that benefit society.
