History

TechCon began in 2012 as a community meetup for engineers and designers. Over the years it has expanded into a multi-day conference featuring talks, workshops and hands-on sessions focused on software engineering, product design and data science.

Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive space where practitioners from startups and large organisations can share practical knowledge, learn new skills, and build meaningful professional relationships.
