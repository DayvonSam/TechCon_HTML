History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small meetup and has grown into a major annual conference showcasing innovation across software, data and design. Each year we host speakers from startups and large enterprises to share practical knowledge.

Mission

Our mission is to create an inclusive learning space where technologists of all levels can connect, learn, and collaborate on real-world challenges.
